MyHealth Passport

MyHealthPassport app solves the problem that we are encountering nowadays, like when someone have to save their medical data but they don't want their medical data to be seen by others. This android app includes firebase authentication for authentication and cloud firestore for storing the medical data of patient which can be saved and accessed using a medicalID.
